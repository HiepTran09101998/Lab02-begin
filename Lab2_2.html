Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a repcipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredents

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  • 1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  • 2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  • 3. Onece butter has melted add cream and eggs
  • 4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the eggs of the pan to the center 20 seconds (as if you making an omelette)
  • 5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served
